About the Book

Grit, prophecy, and an engine that doesn’t just run—it remembers.

“Prophecy speaks of a storm—
a curse bound to blood and steel.
A fury that awakens when darkness dares rise again.”

In Detroit, where industry once built empires and rust now keeps its own counsel, a legacy sleeps beneath chrome and shadow. A generational curse coils around a 1972 Sport Fury—not as a relic, but as a warning with headlights.

Arlene Cunningway thought the past was just a story families tell to survive the present. But some bloodlines don’t inherit heirlooms—they inherit consequences. When the city’s old violence stirs, Arlene is pulled into a lineage of murder, metal, and something far older than rumor.

About J.C. Johnson

J.C. Johnson writes dark thrillers where the supernatural hides in plain sight—inside family histories, industrial echoes, and the quiet lies people tell themselves to sleep. Their stories braid generational secrets with dread-soaked atmosphere, leaving readers with the sense that something is following… just outside the headlights.

If you like your suspense rusted, intimate, and sharp—welcome in. The door closes softly. The lock clicks loud.
